    Bio

    I am a Computer Science PhD student at Tulane University, USA. I completed my BSc from Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), Bangladesh, in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) in 2020, working with Prof. Dr. Muhammad Aminul Haque Akhand. I completed my MSc from the same department and university in 2023, working with Prof. Dr. Kazi Md. Rokibul Alam.

    My current work focuses on designing efficient zero-knowledge-proof (ZKP) based Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) solutions for faster transaction processing in blockchain systems.

    Research Interests

    Blockchain, quantum computing, information security, cryptography, quantum cryptography, and zero-knowledge proofs.
