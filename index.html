    I am Computer Science PhD student at Tulane University, USA. I completed my BSc from the Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), Bangladesh, in the Department of Compuer Science and Engeeniering (CSE) in 2020, working with Prof. Dr. Muhammad Aminul Haque Akhand. After that, I complete my MSc from the same department and same niversity in 2023, working with Prof. Dr. Kazi Md. Rokibul Alam. My research interests lie in the field of Blockchain, Quantum Computing, Information Security, Cryptography, and Quantum Cryptography.

    My current work focuses on using designing an efficient solution for Zero-Knowledge-Proof (ZKP) base Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) for faster transaction processing in the domain of Blockchain Technology. At Tulane, I work under the Supervision of Prof. Lu Peng.
